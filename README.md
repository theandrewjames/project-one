# Shopazon
[Check out Shopazon on Heroku](http://glacial-headland-31476.herokuapp.com)
This website allows you to search for items, buy items and checkout, leave reviews.
## Search for items by name, or type. This will display the items name, item type, author/company and price. 
 ![Search screenshot](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/theandrewjames/shopazon/master/images/shopazon1.png)
## Add items to your cart by clicking on the add to cart button and your shopping cart will populate and update itself.
 ![Add items to cart screenshot](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/theandrewjames/shopazon/master/images/shopazon3.png)
## Checkout by clicking on the checkout button and the payment form will appear and your total is highlighted to the right.
 ![Checkout and pay screenshot](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/theandrewjames/shopazon/master/images/shopazon4.png)
## By clicking the add review button, the review form for that item will appear. Once you write & submit a review it will appear below. 
 ![Add review screenshot](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/theandrewjames/shopazon/master/images/shopazon5.png)
## Once an order has been placed, the previous orders dropdown will populate and allow you to see any previous orders and their information.
 ![Show previous orders screenshot](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/theandrewjames/shopazon/master/images/shopazon6.png)
 ## Upon showing interest in an item by adding it to your cart, the previous orders content area will appeaer and show an item based on item type.
 ![Show recommended items](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/theandrewjames/shopazon/master/images/shopazon7.png)
